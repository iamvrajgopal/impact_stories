From Varsha Nagar MPS to National Champions

Our students of Grade 9 were competing against over 3,000 school children from Grade 8-12 across the country to achieve this feat.

Witness the transformative journey of students from Varsha Nagar MPS, who leveraged problem-solving skills and modern technology to design impactful, real-world solutions for society.

Cultivating Leaders for Tomorrow: The Design Process

This journey was not just about building apps; it was about shifting the students' mindset from being problem-takers to solution-creators. Through a rigorous design thinking process, students learned how to lead self and others, driving solutions with empathy and collaboration.

1. Empathise & Define

Students engaged in deep empathetic listening, identifying critical social challenges faced by communities globally. This skill enabled them to move beyond personal issues to see the wider societal need, fostering self-awareness and social leadership.

2. Ideate & Brainstorm

The teams engaged in intense collaborative brainstorming, leading to the selection of two powerful concepts focused on humanitarian aid. This step fostered team leadership, critical thinking, and disciplined decision-making.

3. Prototype & Test

Students translated their ideas into functional prototypes, applying complex technical skills (AI/ML, Database management) to create tangible solutions. This demonstrated initiative, technical mastery, and the ability to execute a vision.

Two Prototypes, One Vision: Solutions for Conflict Zones

National Finalist & Champion Project

Bridge of Care App: A Digital Lifeline

Students designed a humanitarian digital platform to support refugees and war-displaced individuals, helping them rebuild their lives by connecting them to education, employment, safety, and social networks through an accessible, multilingual, and AI-enabled interface.

Key Technical Integrations:

  • CareBridge (AI Engine): Processes audio/video inputs to create clean, professional CVs for job placements.
  • SafeBridge (Safety Network): Delivers real-time location-based alerts, safety reporting, and geotagging for relief center verification.
  • Technical Stack: Utilized TensorFlow.js, Tesseract OCR, Vision API for document detection, and OpenAI GPT API for summarization.
Top 10 National Finalist Project

GenZ Card: Verified Aid Distribution

This innovation provides a complete system for secure identification, verification, and financial inclusion for refugees. It acts as a dual-purpose identity card and financial support card, ensuring aid reaches the right person quickly and transparently.

Key Technical Integrations:

  • Dual Functionality: EMV Chip for money transfers and QR Code for identification.
  • GenZ VoiceConnect: Advanced system using a unique voice sample as audio identity, linked to a central registry for retrieval and verification.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Features GPS tracking and data retrieval from a secure central registry for efficient aid delivery.
